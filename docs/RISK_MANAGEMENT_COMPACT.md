# Risk Management Compact

## Purpose

This document establishes binding rules for bankroll management and betting discipline. These rules are NON-NEGOTIABLE during emotional moments, losing streaks, or "sure thing" feelings.

**Sign-off:** By using these models, I agree to follow these rules without exception.

---

## The Three Laws

### Law 1: Never Chase Losses

After a losing bet or losing day:

- ❌ DO NOT increase bet size to "win it back"
- ❌ DO NOT place additional bets outside the normal process
- ❌ DO NOT deviate from Kelly sizing
- ✅ DO take a 15-minute break before next decision
- ✅ DO review if the losing bet had positive CLV (if yes, the process worked)

### Law 2: Trust the Process Over Results

- A bet with +3% edge that loses was still a GOOD bet
- A bet with -2% edge that wins was still a BAD bet
- Short-term results (< 500 bets) are dominated by variance
- CLV is the only reliable metric over small samples

### Law 3: Protect the Bankroll

The bankroll is the business. Without it, there is no opportunity.

- The $1,000 reserve is UNTOUCHABLE
- If active capital drops 25%, STOP and review
- Capital preservation > profit maximization

## Hard Limits

### Per-Bet Limits

| Limit | Value | Absolutely Never Exceed |
|-------|-------|------------------------|
| Maximum single bet | 3% of bankroll | $150 (at $5,000) |
| Maximum high-confidence bet | 4% of bankroll | $200 (at $5,000) |
| Minimum edge to bet | 2% | No exceptions |

### Daily Limits

| Limit | Value | Action When Hit |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| Maximum daily exposure | 10% of bankroll | Stop placing new bets |
| Maximum daily loss | 5% of bankroll | Stop for the day |
| Maximum bets per day | 15 | Prioritize highest edge |

### Weekly Limits

| Limit | Value | Action When Hit |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| Maximum weekly loss | 15% of bankroll | Reduce all bet sizes by 50% |
| Consecutive losing days | 5 | Take 1 day completely off |

### Monthly Limits

| Limit | Value | Action When Hit |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| Maximum monthly loss | 25% of bankroll | STOP all betting, full model review |
| Negative CLV streak | 50 bets | Model recalibration required |

## Pre-Bet Checklist

Before EVERY bet, confirm:

- [ ] Edge ≥ 2% (or 3% for props/ML)
- [ ] Bet size follows Kelly calculation
- [ ] Bet size ≤ 3% of current bankroll
- [ ] Daily exposure limit not exceeded
- [ ] Not chasing a previous loss
- [ ] Odds are best available (checked 3+ books)
- [ ] Line hasn't moved significantly against me
- [ ] Game time > 1 hour away (no rushed decisions)

---

## Stop-Loss Protocol

### Level 1: Caution (Daily loss 5%)

- Stop betting for remainder of day
- Review all losing bets for CLV
- If CLV was positive: variance, continue tomorrow
- If CLV was negative: identify model issue

### Level 2: Warning (Weekly loss 15%)

- Reduce all bet sizes by 50% for one week
- Review model predictions vs actual results
- Check for any data quality issues
- Consider taking 2-3 days completely off

### Level 3: Critical (Monthly loss 25%)

- STOP all betting immediately
- Do not resume until completing full review:
  - [ ] Backtest model on recent data
  - [ ] Check for data leakage or errors
  - [ ] Verify odds data quality
  - [ ] Review all bets for patterns
  - [ ] Consult original research/thesis
- Minimum 1-week cooling off period
- Resume only with documented plan changes

## Emotional State Check

Before any betting session, honestly assess:

### Green Light ✅

- Calm, clear-headed
- Following process feels natural
- Accepting that individual bets may lose
- No external stressors affecting judgment

### Yellow Light ⚠️

- Slightly frustrated from recent losses
- Eager to "get back" to previous bankroll level
- Tired or distracted
- **Action:** Reduce bet sizes by 50%, limit to 5 bets max

### Red Light 🛑

- Angry about losses
- Convinced "this one can't lose"
- Under financial pressure
- Intoxicated or impaired
- **Action:** NO BETTING. Walk away completely.

## Variance Reality Check

### Expected Drawdowns (Even with 3% Edge)

| Sample Size | Probability of Being Down |
|-------------|--------------------------|
| 50 bets | 35% |
| 100 bets | 25% |
| 250 bets | 15% |
| 500 bets | 8% |
| 1000 bets | 3% |

### What 55% Win Rate Actually Looks Like

Over 100 bets at 55% true win rate:

- 10% chance of winning 60+ (feels like genius)
- 10% chance of winning 50 or fewer (feels like failure)
- Most likely: 52-58 wins (feels mediocre)

**The feeling is not the reality. Trust the math.**

## Recovery Protocol

If you violate any rule:

1. **Acknowledge it** - Write down exactly what happened
2. **Stop immediately** - No more bets that session
3. **Analyze why** - What emotional state led to this?
4. **Document** - Add to SESSION_HANDOFF.md
5. **Implement safeguard** - What system change prevents recurrence?
6. **Resume carefully** - Half-size bets for 3 days minimum

## Monthly Review Requirements

On the 1st of each month, complete:

- [ ] Review any rule violations
- [ ] Check bankroll vs starting bankroll
- [ ] Assess emotional state throughout month
- [ ] Adjust any limits if bankroll changed significantly
- [ ] Document lessons learned
