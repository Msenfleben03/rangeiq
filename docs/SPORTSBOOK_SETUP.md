## Bonus Strategy

### General Principles

1. **Read terms carefully** - Wagering requirements, eligible bets, time limits
2. **Don't chase bonus at expense of +EV** - Only bet if you would anyway
3. **Track bonus progress** - Know when bonuses expire
4. **Low-risk bonus clearing** - Use on bets with smallest edge requirement

### Common Bonus Types

**Deposit Match:**

- Deposit X, get X in bonus bets
- Usually 1x wagering requirement
- Strategy: Deposit full match amount, use bonus on normal bets

**Risk-Free Bet:**

- Bet X, if lose get X back as bonus bet
- Strategy: Use on slight underdog (+100 to +150) for max value

**Bonus Bets:**

- Free bets with no cash value
- Only winnings paid (not stake)
- Strategy: Use on underdogs (+200 or higher) for max EV

**Odds Boosts:**

- Enhanced odds on specific bets
- Often +EV after boost
- Strategy: Calculate implied prob vs boosted prob, bet if +EV

## Line Shopping Workflow

### Before Every Bet

1. Identify bet (e.g., Duke -3.5)
2. Check odds at ALL books:
   - DraftKings: -110
   - FanDuel: -108 ✓ BEST
   - BetMGM: -112
   - Caesars: -110
3. Place bet at best odds (FanDuel in this example)
4. Log which book had best odds (for pattern tracking)

### Line Shopping Tools

- **OddsJam** - Aggregates odds across books
- **The Action Network** - Good free comparison
- **Manual check** - Open all apps side by side

### Expected Value of Line Shopping

- Finding -108 vs -110 = 1% value add
- Over 1000 bets at $100 average = $1,000 saved
- Worth the extra 2 minutes per bet

## Avoiding Limitations

### Red Flags That Get You Limited

- ❌ Max betting every play
- ❌ Only betting +EV spots, never recreational
- ❌ Betting immediately when lines move
- ❌ Winning consistently at props
- ❌ Depositing only to withdraw winnings

### Mitigation Strategies

- ✅ Vary bet sizes (don't always max)
- ✅ Mix in some recreational bets (SGPs, parlays)
- ✅ Bet at different times of day
- ✅ Round bet amounts ($47 not $46.83)
- ✅ Don't withdraw immediately after every win
- ✅ Use promos and boosts (looks recreational)

### When Limited

1. Don't contact support (usually makes it worse)
2. Document limitation (max bet amounts, banned markets)
3. Continue using book for line reference
4. Reallocate bankroll to remaining books
5. Consider adding new books
